2. How We Use the Information
We use the collected information to:
create and manage your account
display your streaks and activity

improve the app and user experience
maintain security and prevent misuse
We do not sell your personal data.
3. Where Data Is Stored
Your data is stored in our backend database (Lovable Cloud).
Data may be stored on servers outside your country but is handled in accordance with applicable data protection laws.
4. Data Sharing
We do not share your personal data with third parties, except when necessary to:
operate the app securely
comply with legal obligations
protect our rights or prevent abuse
We currently do not use third-party analytics or advertising services.
5. Uploaded Media
Photos and videos you upload may be displayed according to your account settings (e.g. private or public).
Only upload content that you have the legal right to share.
6. Security
We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data.
Passwords are stored securely and never in plain text.
However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.
7. Your Rights
Within the app, you can:
update your profile information
delete your account, which will remove your data to the extent permitted by law
You may also contact us if you have questions regarding your data.

The app is intended for users 12 years of age and older, in accordance with App Store age classification.
We do not knowingly collect personal data from children below this age.
